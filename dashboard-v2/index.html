About this framework

OIECs are derivatives written on prediction-market event futures. Under a Jacobi diffusion dP = σ√P(1−P) dW, the surface of calls and puts across strikes admits closed-form Greeks and a variance-swap replication that defines the Belief Volatility Index (BVIX): a VIX analogue for the probability itself.
